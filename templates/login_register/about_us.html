Our concern is your well being

Pashe Achi is a web application which is developed and designed to provide benefits through the internet. We intend to build a web-based COVID assistance platform from scratch. At the moment, the entire world is suffering from the COVID-19 pandemic. Since the disease has no known cure, people are continuing to get infected by coming in contact with others who carry the virus, causing the infection rate to grow exponentially with time. As a result, providing adequate medical care has become very difficult, especially in a country like Bangladesh. Moreover, finding a valid plasma donor is undoubtedly difficult. If some of these crucial medical services could be provided online, this will ease the suffering of COVID patients seeking medical attention. Moreover, due to a general lack of knowledge about symptoms and primary home treatment procedures, people often face difficulties. Therefore, a platform is much needed for them where they will be able to get much information about COVID. Thus, we intend to build a web-based COVID assistance platform from scratch. The name of our web platform is "Pashe Achi". This name signifies the connectivity among the users and their trust toward our platform for many necessary services. Our number one priority is to create a dynamic web platform, which means we should be able to add, remove, or update any feature at any time. So, there will be a maintenance platform to achieve this goal.
